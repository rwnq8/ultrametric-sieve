# The Ultrametric Sieve

**A Consilience of Number, Nature, Mind, and Form**

## About

This publication proposes a foundational shift in the representation of integers:
from decimal digit-strings to **valuation vectors** recording prime exponents.
The "ultrametric sieve" reveals that integers form an infinite-dimensional lattice
whose geometry is the product of p-adic Bruhat-Tits trees.
